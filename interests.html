So you want to know what I like! Here goes:

    Games: I like board games and handheld games. I go to board game cafes like Snakes & Lattes and Castle in Toronto with friends. Favourites include the Cards Against Humanity series, The Werewolves of Miller's Hollow, and Betrayal at House on the Hill. As for handheld games I like Sonic the Hedgehog, Pokemon, Animal Crossing, and Dangan Ronpa.
    Comics: My girlfriend and I have a bookshelf dedicated to North American and Japanese comics. In fact, we met because we both liked a webcomic, in fact. I go to the Toronto Comic Arts Festival every year and many of my friends are published or aspiring comic artists. I love reading (or watching) stories that start from drawings and doodles.
    Cosplay: I'm sure you've realized I'm a big ol' nerd by this point. Cosplay is a Japanese portmanteau of the English words "costume" and "play". It means dressing up as a fictional character at events and doing photoshoots, videos, skits, and sometimes contests. I like cosplaying from North American and Japanese comics, bands, video games, TV shows, and movies. I've been cosplaying for the past decade and love going to comic conventions in Ontario and elsewhere.
    Pride: I am in an openly lesbian relationship with a trans woman. Bringing awareness to queer issues and positive representation is a big part of who I am.. I want to raise awareness about the most vulnerable and ignored people in the LGBT community, issues such as transphobia in the media and homeless queer youth. This past year I marched in the Dyke March and the Trans march and I love talking with people who are feminist, sex-positive, and all-inclusive when it comes to queer issues.
    Indulgences: I have a terrible obsession with Lush Cosmetics and buy everything there from dish soap to toothpaste. It's like a coffee addiction, but it's soap. At least I'm clean.
